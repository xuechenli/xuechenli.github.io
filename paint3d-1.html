Paint3D Improvement

Pain3D is a desktop app for drawing by Microsoft. It provides hybrid 2D and 3D editing functionalities. Paint 3D features its lightweight yet smart solutions for 3D model creating and editing.

I was on a centralized user research team. Our team partner with several product teams and provide user research support based on requests. I took the lead in protocol creating, moderating, and analyzing the results of the research studies.

As an ongoing project of product iterations, the Paint 3D team had been improving the usability of Paint 3D, especially solving the issues discovered in past user research.

One of the problem areas that I had worked on was that users struggled to move and edit a 3D model in the app. It was hard for them to learn and utilize.

The goal of this Paint3D improvement project was to provide actionable recommendations and ensure the designed solutions were effectivet to solve the problems.

Success

Visual aid elements and quick tutorial had been added to Paint 3D to help users move a 3D model along the third axis (see image). The quick tutorial also got improved later on.

The 3D view mode name had been updated to be more understandable which led to an increase in using this feature to view a 3D model from different angles.

The ability to move a 3D model in 3D view mode had been added to make editing easier.
