Paint 3D Usability Improvement

While Skype has a good customer foundation and has kept evolving, the user experience of calling and messaging still suffered from a lot of inconsistent flows.The design initiated the conversation about tidying up the system. They identified that a comprehensive examination of the system was the key foundation.
One of the problem areas that I had worked on was that users struggled to move and edit a 3D model in the app.
Moving a 3D model along the z-axis was an especially challenging task.

Many people had trouble learning how to do the task, or they accidentally achieved the task but didn't understand what happened.
To tackle this specific problem and many other Paint 3D problems, our research team did reoccurring in-lab usability studies based on each research request from the design team. I took responsibility for many of the studies throughout the design iterations.

In-lab studies are taken in such an environment. This is not a picture of me but just to show how it looks like. Pic: Medium @Melissa DeCapua.

I had adopted an efficient and effective procedure of how to conduct usability studies. The highlights of this process were:
Well prepared plan

I designed the protocol and adjusted it on stakeholder's feedback. For historical model manipulation issues, I would reuse a set of standard tasks for benchmarking.

Visible progress

I sent out the study schedule and also reminded team members to watch the live stream of each session.

Preliminary results

I delivered preliminary results within 1-2 days after the lab sessions completed.

Comprehensive deliverables

I wrote up comprehensive reports with evidence of participants' quotes, lab computer screenshots, and lab recording video clips.

Paint 3D has got much better at supporting model manipulation across time. Take the model manipulation as an example, there were several great implementations added owning to my research recommendations:
1
Adding aids

Visual aid elements and a quick tutorial was added to Paint 3D to help users move a 3D model along the z-axis.

2
Aids got better

Later on, the quick tutorial also got improved by adding reference objects to make it more understandable.

3
Creating clarity

The "3D view" mode name was updated to be more comprehensive. It led to an increase in using this feature to view a 3D model from different angles.
